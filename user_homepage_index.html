How does it work

KeyKash is a very user friendly application. When you allow us access to your keyboard, you will automatically begin being notified of local deals near you. Example: when you type in the word pizza on your messenger system or any other keyboard friendly application, the word pizza will be highlighted to notify you that KeyKash has a coupon waiting for you to use.
