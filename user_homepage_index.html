KeyKash Membership levels

KeyKash is an application that holds your company's coupons inside its internal database. Anytime someone accesses their IOS keyboard and types in a word related to your coupon, it will automatically notify the consumer of your deal.
We've developed a seamless transition process for the deployment of KeyKash that will allow you to post a coupon without stress or delay. Our dedicated internal team will work with you to establish a specific timeline for complete deployment, and will support you throughout the transition process.
